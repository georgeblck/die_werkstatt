Eine gerechte Flüchtlingsverteilung für Europa

10 Oct, 2017 · by GB · Read in about 1 min · (86 Words)

About this visualization

In September 2015 the European Commission proposed a plan to relocate 120’000 refugees from Greece, Italy & Hungary to all European countries. This was supposed to be done by a mandatory distribution key using objective and quantifiable criteria, consisting of the following variables and weightings: > * the size of the population (40%, positive weight), > * the GDP (40%, positive weight), > * the average number of past asylum applications (10%, negative weight) and > * the unemployment rate (10%, negative weight).
